Astronomy Club!

Space wonders, you are all welcome. Our mission is to bring the wonders of the universe closer to you. Whether you're a seasoned stargazer or a curious beginner, we have something for everyone. We are here to inspire and educate. Join us on a journey through the cosmos as we explore the mysteries of the universe and discover the beauty and awe-inspiring majesty of the stars.

"The atoms of out bodies are tracable to stars that manufactured them in their cores and exploded these enriched ingredients across our galaxy, billions of years ago. For this reason, we are biologically connected to every other living thing in the world. We are chemically connected to all molecules on Earth. And we are atomically connected to all atoms in the universe. We are not figuratively, but literally connected to the universe".

~ Neil deGrasse Tyson
